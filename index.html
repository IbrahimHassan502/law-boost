don't take our Word for it — it's science!

Spaced repetition has been proven to increase the rate of learning, and increase bar exam passage rates. It works by frequently testing new and difficult concepts, while older and less difficult concepts are tested less to exploit the psychological spacing effect.

Law School Boost will automagically optimize your study session and focus your time on learning concepts you struggle with most while ensuring that easier topics are not forgotten.
